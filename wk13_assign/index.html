Morton Poetry Prize from Sarabande Books and went on to win the PEN Center USA Literary Award. It was the only book of poetry included in the San Francisco Chronicle's Best Books list in 2000. His book, Method Writing (Bombshelter Press), is in its 11th edition. He has authored over a dozen collections including The Naked Eye: New and Selected Poems, 1987-2012.
